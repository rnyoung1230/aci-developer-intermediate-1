Dogs

German shepherds are a breed of dogs that originated in Germany. They are large and muscular and typically have a black and tan coat. Some have a solid black coat. German shepherds are intelligent and loyal dogs.

Cats

Siamese cats originated in Thailand and are a breed of short-haired cats. They are medium-sized and have a distinct color pattern. The color of their face, ears, legs, and tails are darker than the rest of their body. Siamese cats can live up to 20 years.
